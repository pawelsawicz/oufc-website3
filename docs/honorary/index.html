Deceased honorary members

Dr Stanislaw Komorowski

1953-2010

Undersecretary of State, Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Warsaw, Poland He was listed on the flight carrying the President of Poland Lech Kaczynski which crashed near Smolensk, Russia, on 10 April 2010, killing all aboard. He was buried on April 16, 2010 at Powazki Cemetery in Komorowski’s family grave. He was decorated, posthumously, with the Order of Polonia Restituta.

Mr Ryszard Kaczorowski

1919-2010

The last émigré President of the Republic of Poland. He was listed on the flight carrying the President of Poland Lech Kaczynski and 95 others, which crashed near Smolensk, Russia, on 10 April 2010, killing all aboard.
